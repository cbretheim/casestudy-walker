Fourth Wall

By Steve Marsh

Investigating the Magnetic Field that Drew the Staves and yMusic to Eau Claire

Mapping the Bon Iver universe can feel a daunting task, better left to string theorists, genetic anthropologists, and Pitchfork editors. But by listening to the Staves and yMusic's collaboration The Way is Read, your mind can begin to perceive a vibration, maybe even a vector - if not precisely a fixed point - upon which to begin a sonic investigation into what the hell is going on out there in Eau Claire, Wisconsin
